Chris Ward

I'm a UX, UI, product and engineering solution expert and consultant.

I have over 20 years of experience working with the web, including over 10 as a consultant, and am passionate about helping people deliver solutions that make everyone's lives easier.

Asking the right questions to ascertain the 'why' behind the 'what' is the key to ensuring a final product fulfils its potential, whatever the project.

I am an expert at translating conflicting needs, limitations, and jargon of multiple stakeholders. Whether it's end users or different internal stakeholders, it's ultimately about planning a solution that satisfies everyone, whether introducing web technologies to the prehistoric world of financial services or bringing a more analytic and evidence-based approach to the product strategy in e‑commerce.

My background in full-stack development plus web, graphic, and UX design provides me with a unique and wide-ranging skill set that lets me see projects from multiple perspectives. After starting my own business, working with startups going to market, and with multinationals with established offerings, I have a wealth of experience with the different stages of the product life cycle and varied scales of company growth.

I can help you and your users get where you both want to be.
