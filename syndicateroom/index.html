Designing scalable investment journeys

At SyndicateRoom, I was responsible for designing and implementing modular dashboards and investment flows for thousands of investors. By combining user research, journey mapping, and reusable UX/UI patterns, we built a system that supported rapid growth, improved retention, and enabled the fund to grow from scratch to £77M deployed.

← Back

Problem

  • No structured design system.
  • Investment process was inconsistent and hard to navigate.
  • Needed scalable flows to support rapid growth.

Process

  • Research: Stakeholder interviews, personas, journey mapping.
  • Design: Wireframes, prototypes, iterative usability testing.
  • System: Modular dashboards, investment flow patterns, component library.
  • Team Growth: Expanded from 2 → 6, mentoring designers & developers.

Solution

  • Designed dashboards for 2,000+ investors.
  • Reusable, modular UX/UI system for scalability.
  • Structured upskilling and mentoring program.

Impact

  • Grew fund from scratch to £77M deployed.
  • Achieved 76% customer retention.
  • Team efficiency and output scaled significantly.
